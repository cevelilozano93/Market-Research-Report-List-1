<p><p>The breast cancer testing market encompasses three primary types: screening tests, diagnostic tests, and monitoring tests. Screening tests, such as mammograms, are used for early detection in asymptomatic women. Diagnostic tests, including biopsies and imaging studies, help confirm the presence and type of cancer in symptomatic individuals. Monitoring tests track the progression or recurrence of cancer following treatment, using methods like blood tests or imaging scans. Together, these tests play a crucial role in the comprehensive management of breast cancer.</p></p>
